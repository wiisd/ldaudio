Projects

Blood Removal Service

Blood Removal Service is an ongoing project for my Honours Degree at Abertay University. With a focus on creating a violence experience for the player without using heavy gore or viscera, I'm leaning heavily into my audio and visual effects skills to achieve my intended game feel.

Holiday Hooligans

A short, fun 'Where's Wally' type game created for the Global Game Jam 2026. Me and a team of 6 others designed and developed it in under 48 hours. The theme being 'mask', the player must find the wanted hooligan based on the mask they wear while roaming the holiday island. For this project I undertook all audio design and implementation alongside other mixed design roles.

Playable now!

FOOTSTEPPER

Footstepper is a prototype of an audio creation tool I developed for an audio class during uni. It comes with over 100 high quality footsteps sounds and allows the user to modulate and tweak the footsteps to suit their needs. With further development I would like to bring this from just a demonstration to a full piece of software.
