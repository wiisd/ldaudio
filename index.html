Projects

JOHN

JOHN is a point-and-click, narrative focused game created by me and a team of 7 others for our professional development class at university, where we were mentored by The Chinese Room.
I was the sole audio designer on the team and handled design, creation and implementation of the sound effects.

Playable now! Or, watch the gameplay video

Blood Removal Service

Blood Removal Service is a project created for my Honours Degree at Abertay University. With a focus on creating a violence experience for the player without using heavy gore or viscera, I leaned heavily into my audio and visual effects skills to achieve my intended game feel.

Holiday Hooligans

A short, fun 'Where's Wally' type game created for Global Game Jam 2026. Me and a team of 6 others designed and developed it in under 48 hours. The theme being 'mask', the player must find the wanted hooligan based on the mask they wear while roaming the holiday island.
For this project I undertook all audio design and implementation alongside other mixed design roles.

Playable now! Or, watch the gameplay video!

FOOTSTEPPER

Footstepper is a prototype of an audio creation tool I developed for an audio class during uni. It comes with over 100 high quality footsteps sounds and allows the user to modulate and tweak the footsteps to suit their needs.
With further development I would like to bring this from just a demonstration to a full piece of software by allowing importing and exporting of custom sounds, alongside additional provided assets and effects.
